About YourNextWatchUnder1K

Why this website ?

Last year, I was on a business trip to Paris and wanted to buy my dad a decent watch as gift. But, I was on a tight budget, and somehow I had this perception that good watches always came with expensive price tags.

I started by searching for "best men's watches under X" or "top watches under Y," trying to make the most of my money.
Little by little, I got involved in watch communities, went through a lot of buyer’s guides, and—most importantly—was introduced to some great affordable brands like Timex, Seiko, and Orient. Shortly after that, I caught what my mother called "watch fever," and damn, this hobby is addictive! After a few "just one more watch" moments, I ended up with a modest but affordable watch collection.

So, why this website? Well, I recently started a web development course, The Odin Project, and one assignment asked us to build a landing page and share it for peer review. Instead of using random text and images, I thought, Why not compile everything I’ve learned about my new hobby into a single page as a buyer’s guide? This way, I could share some of the best watches to consider when picking your next timepiece.
And that’s how "Your Next Watch Under $1K" was born.
